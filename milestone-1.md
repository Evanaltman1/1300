## Design Rationale
> Explain why your design is appropriate for your audience.
> Specially, why does your content organization, navigation, and site design/layout meet the goals of your users?
> How did you employ familiarity (i.e. design patterns) to improve the usability of the site for your audience? (2-4 sentences)

The site is well-organized with easily recognizable pages such as Home, About, Services, FAQ, and Contact, so parents can quickly find information about the provider's qualifications, services, and how to get in touch without having to learn anything new. The About page has the provider's credentials, education, and work experience all in one place, while the Services page is separated into therapy and psychological testing so parents can confirm the provider is able to handle their child's specific needs. The site also uses familiar design patterns like a hamburger menu on mobile and a horizontal navigation bar on desktop, making it easy and intuitive to use. An FAQ page further helps by answering common questions upfront, reducing anxiety and making parents feel more confident before reaching out.
