Licensed psychological services for children and adolescents in New York City.

Welcome

Welcome to the practice of Dr. Bonnie Altman, a licensed psychologist based in New York City with over 21 years of experience working with children, adolescents, and families.

If you are a parent searching for support for your child, you are in the right place. Dr. Altman specializes in working with teenagers and has spent her career helping young people navigate anxiety, depression, stress, and the everyday challenges of growing up.

Dr. Altman offers one-on-one counseling in a warm, confidential, and supportive environment. Whether your child is struggling with their mental health, having a hard time at school, or just needs someone to talk to, Dr. Altman is here to help.
