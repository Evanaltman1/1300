## Interactivity Usability Justification
> Explain how the interactivity _functionally_ improves the user's experience and helps them accomplish their goals. (i.e. Your interactivity does _more_ than add additional clicks; the interactivity doesn't insert additional barriers for the user when working towards their goals.)
> Explain how your interactivity's design effectively uses affordances, visibility, feedback, and familiarity.
> Write a paragraph (3-4 sentences)

The hamburger menu allows parents visiting the site on a mobile device to easily access all pages without the navigation taking up valuable screen space, keeping the focus on the content. The menu employs strong affordances through the familiar ☰ icon, provides immediate visual feedback by revealing a fullscreen navigation overlay when clicked, and follows the familiar design pattern users expect from mobile websites. The modal on the Services page allows parents to learn more about psychological testing without leaving the page, reducing friction in their research process. The modal uses a clear close button in the top right corner for familiarity, a dark overlay for visibility, and an obvious open button with descriptive text for affordance.
